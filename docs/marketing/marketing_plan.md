# Hermit KMS: Marketing Plan

## Project Overview
**Product**: Hermit KMS  
**Vision**: To be the most secure and developer-friendly secret management platform for organizations that prioritize both security and agility.

## Target Audience
1. **The Infrastructure Gatekeeper (DevOps/SRE)**: Focused on reducing secret sprawl, automating rotation, and maintaining high availability.
2. **The Compliance Champion (CISO/Security Lead)**: Needs audit trails, fine-grained access control, and industry-standard encryption (HashiCorp Vault).
3. **The Workflow-First Developer**: Wants secrets to "just work" in their terminal and local environment without manual `.env` file management.
4. **The Growing SaaS Company**: Needs a multi-tenant solution that scales from 1 team to 100 teams without complexity.

## Messaging & Positioning
- **Primary Tagline**: "The Fortress for Your Secrets, Built for Your Developers."
- **Positioning Statement**: Hermit KMS provides the industrial-grade security of HashiCorp Vault with a streamlined developer experience and a superior IAM policy engine.
- **Key Pillars**:
    - **Uncompromising Security**: 3-tier protection and URN-based policies.
    - **Developer Delight**: CLI-native, `hermit run` injection, and modern web UI.
    - **Enterprise Ready**: Multi-tenant, audit-logged, and version-controlled.

## Marketing Channels
- **Community & Social**:
    - **GitHub**: Maintain a high-quality README, active issue tracking, and detailed documentation.
    - **Hacker News & Reddit**: "Show HN" and targeted posts in `r/devops` and `r/security`.
    - **X/Twitter**: Daily tips on secret management and security best practices.
- **Content Marketing**:
    - **Technical Blog**: "Why your IAM policy needs to be URN-based," "Stop committing .env files."
    - **Whitepapers**: "Achieving SOC2 compliance with Hermit KMS."
- **Direct Engagement**:
    - **Product Hunt Launch**: Coordinated launch with a focus on the developer community.
    - **Demo Webinars**: Bi-weekly live sessions showing "Terminal to Production" workflows.
